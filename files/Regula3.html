ASTROWORLD FOOD FOUNDATION

INGREDIENTS

2 oz. silver tequila

1 oz. Cointreau

1 oz. lime juice

coarse salt

DIRECTIONS

Chill a cocktail glass, and then rub its rim with lime juice and dip it in coarse salt.

Add tequila, Cointreau, lime juice, and ice together in a cocktail shaker.

Shake and then strain into the glass over ice.


For a slighter sweeter version, add agave syrup before shaking. And of course, if you prefer them frozen (though that's not the traditional way to make a margarita), simply add the ingredients to a blender and then blend until smooth.
